  1. Cats eat mice.
  2. Mice have got limited line of sight, cats haven't.
  3. Every eighth turn cats need to sleep one turn to recover their strength.
  4. Having eaten a mouse, the cat needs to sleep one turn as well.
  5. Dogs do nothing, apart from saving nearby mice from cats (not on purpose).
  6. Mice and cats move one cell in each direction, dogs two cells.
  7. Game has no goal or end, it just runs.
